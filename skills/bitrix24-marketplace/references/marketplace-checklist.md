# Marketplace Submission Checklist (Condensed)

Source: `/Users/just/Sites/ofs/b24.loc/.docs/b24_market/2026-02-24_tofs_gtd_market_submission_checklist.md`

## Technical readiness

- Install/update/uninstall tested.
- Rollback tested on clean stand.
- No leftover dev artifacts in package.

## Quality and security

- No PHP warnings/fatals in key flows.
- No JS console errors in core UI.
- Rights and OAuth checks enforced.
- Logs stored safely, no public debug toggles.

## Localization and UX

- Full RU localization; other locales if targeted.
- No hardcoded portal paths.

## Marketplace card

- Accurate title/slug/category.
- Description includes limits and requirements.
- Screenshots and icon match real UI.
- Changelog prepared.

## Legal

- Privacy policy and license/offer published.
- Partner agreement and sales settings for paid apps.
